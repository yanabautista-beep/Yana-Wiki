# Metric Definitions

## CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)

**Definition:** Total ad spend divided by the number of new customers or bookings acquired, per channel.

**Current benchmark:** AED X per acquisition (target: AED Y). Last updated: MM.YYYY.

**How it's calculated:** Total spend ÷ confirmed acquisitions (bookings/installs attributed to paid).

---

## ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)

**Definition:** Revenue generated divided by total ad spend. Measures efficiency of paid campaigns.

**Current benchmark:** X (target: Y). Last updated: MM.YYYY.

**How it's calculated:** Revenue attributed to ads ÷ total spend.

---

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)

**Definition:** Total marketing spend (paid + organic costs) divided by new customers acquired in the period.

**Current benchmark:** AED X per customer (target: AED Y). Last updated: MM.YYYY.

**How it's calculated:** Total marketing spend ÷ new customers.

---

## CTR (Click Through Rate)

**Definition:** Percentage of impressions that result in a click, tracked per channel and creative.

**Current benchmark:** X% on Meta, Y% on Google. Last updated: MM.YYYY.

**How it's calculated:** Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100.

---

## App Installs

**Definition:** Number of new app installs attributed to paid and organic channels per period.

**Current benchmark:** X installs/month (target: Y). Last updated: MM.YYYY.

**How it's calculated:** Tracked via attribution tool (Adjust / AppsFlyer / Firebase).

---

## Email Open Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of delivered emails opened by recipients, per campaign.

**Current benchmark:** X% (industry benchmark: ~20%). Last updated: MM.YYYY.

**How it's calculated:** Unique opens ÷ Delivered emails × 100.

---

## Organic Sessions

**Definition:** Website sessions attributed to unpaid organic search (Google/Bing).

**Current benchmark:** X sessions/month (target: Y). Last updated: MM.YYYY.

**How it's calculated:** Google Analytics — organic channel, sessions metric.

---

## Conversion Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of sessions or app visits that result in a completed booking.

**Current benchmark:** X% (target: Y%). Last updated: MM.YYYY.

**How it's calculated:** Bookings ÷ Sessions × 100, by channel.
