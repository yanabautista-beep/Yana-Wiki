# HC10 vs AED 50 Off Discount Test

## Hypothesis

The AED 50 Off deal would drive higher booking volume and comparable second-month retention vs the HC10 voucher, justifying higher CPAs.

## Mechanic

- **Control:** HC10 voucher (10% off Home Cleaning)
- **Treatment:** AED 50 Off flat discount
- **Channel:** Meta paid campaigns
- **Analysis:** Cohort analysis on booking rates and CPA

## Results

| Metric | HC10 | AED 50 Off | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Meta CPA | $96.25 | $212.00 | +120% |
| Month 2 Booking Rate | 25% | 23% | -2pp |
| Voucher Usages (weekend) | — | 98 (down from 198) | -50% |
| Discount-to-GMV | baseline | -5.68% | ↓ |
| PC1 | Negative | +3.13% | ↑ |

## Next Step

Reverted to HC10 deal. AED 50 Off was harder to scale — higher CPA made budget increases inefficient, and voucher usage dropped significantly.

## Status

Resolved — HC10 reinstated as primary voucher. Decision made August 2025.
